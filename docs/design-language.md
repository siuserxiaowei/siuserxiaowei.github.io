# Design Language

## Positioning

This is a personal portfolio for "小白学 AI": a beginner-to-builder learning log with real artifacts.

## Visual Direction

- Blend: knowledge constellation + research archive + blueprint console.
- Tone: honest, practical, slightly technical, not guru-like.
- Primary colors: ink green `#07130f`, paper `#f7f5ea`.
- Accents: learning green `#00e6a8`, electric blue `#63d8ff`, proof lime `#d8ff6a`.
- Typography: serif display for human warmth, clean sans for project details, monospace for system labels.

## Rules

- The homepage must quickly answer: who I am, what I am learning, what I have built.
- Every featured project needs a repo link or demo link.
- Avoid fake numbers, fake client logos, fake senior-expert claims.
- Cards are only for repeated project items; sections are full-width bands.
